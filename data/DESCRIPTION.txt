Site covariate data:
Site covariate data was downloaded and mapped into ArcMap. In ArcMap, the site covariate features from each dataset below were selected where they overlapped with a COLO site. 

National Land Cover Database (NLCD):
2016 data available for download at: https://www.usgs.gov/centers/eros/science/national-land-cover-database
Land Cover types grouped together by category as described in the manuscript. 
Percent canopy cover used as a continuous variable (centered and scaled)
